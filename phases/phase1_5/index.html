üéØ What Are Materiality Topics?

Materiality topics are ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) issues that significantly impact your business operations, stakeholder relationships, and long-term value creation. Understanding these topics is crucial for making informed sustainability decisions.

In the next phase, your strategic choices will directly influence your company's performance across these materiality areas, affecting your ESG scores, operational costs, and ultimately your market valuation.
